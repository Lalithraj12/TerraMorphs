📜 Rulebook & Player Guide

🎯 Game Objective

You are a scientist on an exoplanet. Engineer species, conquer deadly environments, and terraform the world for life. Complete all modules to succeed.

⚙️ Game Modules & Flow

  1. 🌍 Planet Naming – Name your world. Stored as planetName.
  2. 🧬 Genome Editor – Select zone-specific traits. Autosaved to finalTraits.
  3. ⚙️ BioForge – Validate traits against environmental constraints. Fix mismatches.
  4. 🔧 Trait Drafting – Filter, organize, and prioritize your trait set for synthesis.
  5. 🧬 DNA Synthesis – Build your DNA strand visually. Adds synergy and adaptation bonuses.
  6. 🧢 Evolution Trials – Test species against hazards with real-time energy and cooldowns.
  7. 🌱 Terraforming – Apply your traits to change planet conditions. Reach ≥100% to win.
  8. 📄 Final Result – Review your mission report with survival score and conditions.

🧪 Controls & Shortcuts

  • 👣 Movement: W / A / S / D
  • 🖱 Interact: Walk near equipment to trigger module
  • 📊 Open Dashboard: Tab
  • 📖 Open Rulebook: Ctrl + H
  • 🔁 Restart Game: localStorage.clear() in Dev Console

🔍 Module Details

🌍 Planet Naming

Give your planet a unique name. Used in final reports.

🧬 Genome Editor

Select traits needed for each survival zone. Traits are auto-saved and shown in your DNA profile later.

⚙️ BioForge

Check trait compatibility with each environment. Fix or swap traits before moving forward.

🔧 Trait Drafting

Reorganize, remove, or filter traits based on importance. Synergize trait pairs for bonuses.

🧬 DNA Synthesis

Assemble your final DNA strand. Traits are animated and boost stats depending on synergy.

🧢 Evolution Trials

Survive 3 hazard zones in real time. Use energy strategically, dodge threats, and gain a survival score.

🌱 Terraforming

Each round simulates environmental change using your traits. Log progress and aim for 100% terraforming success.

📄 Final Result

View a detailed report of your species’ survival, conditions, and colonization success. You can export it as an image.

🧭 Gameplay Tips

  • 💡 Use multi-zone traits to save DNA space.
  • ⚠️ Avoid contradictory traits in BioForge (e.g. cold vs. heat resistance).
  • 🧬 Balance trait strength, don’t overload one category.
  • 🎯 Prioritize critical zones during Evolution Trials.
  • 🌱 Terraforming only unlocks after successful trials (≥70% survival).
  • 💾 Your progress is automatically saved via localStorage.
  • 🔍 Hover near lab modules for tips (if enabled).

🏁 Completion & Outcome

  • ✅ You succeed if terraforming reaches 100%.
  • 📈 Bonus for survival score ≥ 70% in Evolution Trials.
  • 🖼 Final Result page offers summary and screenshot export.
  • 🏆 Replay to unlock new trait combos and get better outcomes!
